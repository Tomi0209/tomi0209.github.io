Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

HagioDistrict (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. The app is designed to support personal reflection, exercises, and learning based on principles of hagiotherapy. It is not a medical or psychological service.

1. Data We Collect

HagioDistrict collects the minimum data required to provide the app’s core functionality:

  • Name (provided during registration)
  • Email address (used for login and account access)
  • Password (encrypted/hashed)
  • Favorites / saved items (user-generated preferences)
  • Custom programs or notes (user-generated content)
  • In-app purchase status (premium, workbook unlock, etc.)

We do NOT collect:

  • Location data
  • Health or sensitive personal data
  • Contacts, photos, calendar, or files from your device
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or tracking for ad profiling

2. Purpose of Data Use

Your data is only used for essential app features, including:

  • Account creation and login
  • Saving personalized content and favorites
  • Managing premium access or purchased workbooks
  • Providing core app functionality

3. Data Sharing

Your data is never sold, shared, or used for advertising. It is only stored securely to operate your account.

4. Data Security

Passwords are stored securely using industry-standard hashing. Access to the database is restricted and protected.

5. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Correct inaccurate information
